XRK Tools — Frequently Asked Questions
This FAQ covers the public XRK Tool Suite.
Paid Edition details are intentionally limited for security.
What is XRK Tools?
XRK Tools is a collection of lightweight, fast utilities designed to make schoolwork and everyday computing easier.
The Free Edition is available here on GitHub.
What platforms are supported?
Windows: Supported

Linux: Coming soon

ChromeOS (Linux container): Coming soon

macOS: Not planned for Free Edition

Paid Edition includes full cross‑platform support.
What’s the difference between Free Edition and Paid Edition?
Free Edition:

Bare‑bones features

No updater

No GUI

No cross‑platform builds

Rare updates

Bug fixes only
Paid Edition:

Full features

GUI versions

Updater Pro

Cross‑platform builds

Weekly updates

Premium tools
Why does the Free Edition get outdated?
The Free Edition is meant to be a simple, stable demo of XRK Tools.
It receives bug fixes, but new features are reserved for the Paid Edition.
Will the Free Edition ever get new features?
Yes — but rarely, usually when XRK jumps to a new generation (Gen 7, Gen 8, etc.).
These updates are always bare‑bones.
Where can I download the Free Edition?
All Free Edition downloads are available in the Releases section of this public repo.
Where can I get the Paid Edition?
Paid Edition downloads are handled through a private updater and private repository, That said please email Xrk_interactive@gmail.com for info on payment.
Details are not public for security reasons.
Can I see the source code?
No. XRK Tools is closed‑source.
Can I request features?
Yes — open an Issue on this repo with the Free Edition label.
Who is XRK Tools for?
Students, teachers, and anyone who wants fast, simple utilities for organizing files, taking notes, and checking system info.
